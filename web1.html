About the System

The Crime Management System is designed to help law enforcement agencies efficiently manage and track crime reports, complaints, and related activities.

Features

  • Secure user authentication for both officials and citizens.
  • Real-time tracking of complaints and investigations.
  • Data analytics to identify crime trends and patterns.
  • Easy access to case files and evidence documentation.
